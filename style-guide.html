Writing Rules

Sentence Construction

  • Use active voice.
  • Keep sentences short.
  • One idea per sentence.
  • Start with the action or subject.

Preferred Terminology

  • "losses" not "damages"
  • "hack" not "cyber incident"
  • "scam" not "fraudulent scheme"
  • "wallet" not "digital wallet"
  • "MEV bot" not "maximal extractable value bot"
  • "exploit" not "vulnerability exploitation"

Banned Phrases

  • absolutely devastating
  • you won't believe
  • shocking revelation
  • in this day and age
  • needless to say
  • it goes without saying
  • I think / We believe / In my opinion

Formatting Standards

Emoji Usage

  • 🚨 Emergency/Alert
  • ⚠️ Warning
  • 📉 Statistics
  • 📊 Analysis
  • 🔍 Investigation

Bullet Rules

  • Use bullets for statistics, sequences, action items, and key facts.
  • Do not use bullets for narrative explanations.
  • Keep bullet structure parallel and short.

Quality Checklist

  • Numbers are accurate and sourced
  • No speculation presented as fact
  • Headline under 100 characters
  • Opening summarizes everything
  • Professional, factual, educational tone
